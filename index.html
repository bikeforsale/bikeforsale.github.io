I'm a data scientist who does other things too.

Public health surveillance

I co-founded Hyfe with the intention of scaling up infectious disease surveillance so that public health authorities can monitor cough frequency variation in space-time.

Research and academia

I do public health, epidemiology, and health economics research. I did my PhD at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam / the University of Barcelona. I did my MPH at the Copenhagen School of Global Health / the Andalusian School of Public Health.

Most of my research is in malaria and tuberculosis.

Consulting

I do some consulting in data science and data management. My company: databrew.cc.
